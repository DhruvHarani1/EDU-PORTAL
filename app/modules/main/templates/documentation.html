What is EduPortal?

EduPortal is a cloud-native school management system designed to streamline operations for faculty, administrators, and students. By centralizing data and automating routine tasks like timetable generation and attendance tracking, EduPortal allows educational institutions to focus on what matters most: teaching and learning.

Key Features

  • Unified Dashboard: Single sign-on for all stakeholders.
  • Real-time Sync: Attendance updates reflect instantly across the platform.
  • Secure: Enterprise-grade encryption for sensitive student data.
